About Me

Hi! My name is Prishanti Moonsamy and I would like to welcome you to my little corner of the world.

I am currently working as a singer at a little jazz club in Johannesburg while also trying to build a career as an artist outside of the club.

This space is where I share my thoughts and experiences and where I hope to share little slices of my life as I grow as both a person and an artist. In time I hope to be able to see myself grow into someone that is happier, healthier, driven and be able to look at myself and be pround of where I am and where I started.

I want to be able to use this platform, while it might not be anything revolutionary, to speak about anything and everything that is important to me. What I witness everyday being a women in a space that is dominated by men, not even professionally but physically and the trials and tribulations that come with that.

I think that this space can really be something beautiful and inspiring if nothing else, something to look back on and think, "Wow...you've really come a long way, kid".

So thanks for hanging out with me on this whackly little journey :)
